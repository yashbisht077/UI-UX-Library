NAVBAR

Navbar is a navigation bar that is used to navigate between different pages of a website.

Modern Minimalist Navbar

A clean and simple navigation bar with just the brand name and links. Ideal for minimal and distraction-free designs.

MyBrand
  • Home
  • About
  • Services
  • Contact

HTML

                    
<nav class="navbar modern-minimalist" ">
  <div class="navbar-brand">MyBrand</div>
  <ul class="navbar-links">
  <li><a href="#">Home</a></li>
  <li><a href="#">About</a></li>
  <li><a href="#">Services</a></li>
  <li><a href="#">Contact</a></li>
</ul>
